Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Tomato
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Butter

Instructions:

1. Cook bacon until crispy.

2. Slice the tomato thinly.

3. Butter one side of each bread slice.

4. Place a slice of cheese on the unbuttered side of a bread slice.

5. Add cooked bacon and tomato slices on top of the cheese.

6. Top with another slice of bread, buttered side facing out.

7. Heat a skillet over medium heat.

8. Place the sandwich in the skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides, and the cheese is melted.

9. Serve hot and enjoy!
